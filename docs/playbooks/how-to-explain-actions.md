# How to Explain Actions

Explain notable actions before or while taking them, but keep it short.

## Pre-action pattern

- what you are about to do
- why that is the right next step
- what signal you expect back

## Post-action pattern

- what you did
- result
- any remaining risk or uncertainty

Avoid narrating every tiny step.
